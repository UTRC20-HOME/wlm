WLM Token: Overview & Strengths

WLM (Wait List Miners Token) is designed to drive long-term stability and growth in the blockchain ecosystem. Built on the Tron blockchain with the UTRC20 token standard, WLM aims to create a decentralized, sustainable, and collaborative environment for token holders.

  • Patience-Driven Philosophy: Long-term growth with steady returns.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Merges smaller tokens into larger liquidity pools.
  • Innovative Marketing: YouTube songs to promote partner projects.
  • Scalability: Tron blockchain ensures low-cost, fast transactions.

About UTRC20

UTRC20 simplifies liquidity pooling for token creators. It allows seamless swaps and high APY staking opportunities. Join a unified ecosystem where smaller tokens thrive together.

Wait List Miners Platform Overview

Wait List Miners offers a unique mining platform where users can participate in mining through a 2-year locked contract with a guaranteed 4% APY. Miners earn UTRC20 tokens as rewards and help strengthen the ecosystem by providing additional collateral to liquidity pools.

  • Minimum entry: 1 TRX
  • Guaranteed 4% APY
  • Lending and borrowing features to enhance participation
